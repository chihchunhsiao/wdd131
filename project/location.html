Penghu Location

Penghu is a group of islands located in the Taiwan Strait, between the main island of Taiwan and mainland China. Penghu Islands have 90 large and small islands.

Penghu Island is the largest island in the Penghu Islands. The Tianren Chrysanthemum is the symbolic flower of the island. Penghu Island is also known as Chrysanthemum Island.

Qimei Island is famous for its double heart stone weir which is a popular attraction in Penghu. Stone Weir is a kind of fishing trap in the intertidal zone. These stone walls were made by coral reef and basalt. Double-heart stone weir is the most complete and romantic stone weir in the world.

Jibei Island is a popular base for summer vacation in Penghu, and Shazui Bay is especially famous. It is surrounded by rock formations and coral reefs, and the island is also rich in cultural landscapes and cultural sites.
